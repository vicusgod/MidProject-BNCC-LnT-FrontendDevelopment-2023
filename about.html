About Us

BNCC Learning & Training

Bina Nusantara Computer Club is a computer-based organization in Binus University and we are a family of excellent youth who work passionately and professionally.

Established in 1989, BNCC has now reached its 33rd year. It has been a long milestone for us to create a strong foundation and belief to learn and forge ourselves with computer, technology business, and organizational skills.

BNCC educating others in computer and organizational knowledge, having technology products such as software house and online media, doing technology research, and creating technology communities inside and outside the university. Be successful by joining us. Be a part of our family and relation, and become one of the future generation, a generation which will always give innovations on the technology era.

We provide an online learning platform to learn about coding. We have many types of courses available on our website, from beginner to advanced level. We guarantee the quality of the modules we have through skilled teachers. What are you waiting for, try our course now!
